What We Do

Secure Information Project is a nonprofit LLC organized to conduct research, develop new technologies, and improve systems that affect the data security and personal information of vulnerable populations across the globe.

Through comprehensive research, thoughtful collaboration, and intentional innovation, Secure Information Project will operate at the nexus of how things are now and how things can be. We believe that emerging technology can help solve some of the world's most pernicious problems; we just need to figure out how. That's our mission. As an emerging technology nonprofit, we will learn what is needed, present our research findings to the world, and invite independent thinkers to join us in collaboration as we work to develop systems that work for the benefit of all - not for the benefit of a few.

Research

Modern systems were built using outdated blueprints. Emerging technology has made the systems that govern our daily lives obsolete; from government to supply chain to finance to real estate. New and intentional research can rewrite the blueprints on which our systems operate by leveraging emerging blockchain concepts and applying modern schools of thought that seek to shift control from the few to the many.

Collaboration

Knowledge, information, and understanding should not be owned by a single entity. Secure Information Project seeks to gather partners that believe in open-sourced, free, and equal access to knowledge and information. When we gather what we learn and share that with the world, we can level the balance of power and provide equal opportunity to all.

Development

The challenge is clear: develop methods to support legacy systems as they adapt to and integrate with emerging blockchain technology. Before we can enjoy a decentralized world, we must develop methods that bridge between the old and the new - while designing for the world we want. Development takes many forms, and Secure Information Project will support and advocate for them all.

//Research Designed to Understand and Inform

Research Designed to Understand and Inform

Let's take what we know and throw it all away. What don't we know? If we could learn what we don't know, what could we build then?

​

Research can mean many things. At Secure Information Project, research is a mindset; everything we know must be questioned and challenged. From market research to consumer research, social research to technological research - SIP is interested in dissecting the fundamentals on which our systems are built and figuring out if and how blockchain technology can be leveraged to do things another way.

​

Using contemporary, objective, and open-source research methods, we will explore new territories and create new baseline data for the builders who will assemble the foundations of tomorrow's global systems.

Collaborations That Unlock New Possibilities

At Secure Information Project, we believe in success through teamwork. We are seeking collaborative partners - nonprofit organizations, for-profit businesses, individuals and independent entities - to work with us to share knowledge, pilot new initiatives, advocate for the advancement of blockchain technology, promote understanding through education, and protect information by ensuring it remains free and open to society and all its people.
